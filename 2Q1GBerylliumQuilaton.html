Billie Eilish: The Haunting Visionary

Billie Eilish, was born on December 18, 2001 in Los Angeles. During her teenage years, she rose to fame with her song "Ocean Eyes", from her EP "don't smile at me" that went viral on SoundCloud around 2015, which had started her music career. Writing and recording songs with her brother, Finneas, lead to the release of her debut album "When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?" in 2019.

Her first album mainly talked about her experiences with depression, this album had won several awards, including 5 grammys. She had released her second album, "Happier Than Ever", in 2021, which was about misogyny and how fame can weigh down artists. Her third and latest album "Hit Me Hard And Soft", released in 2024, revisits old elements from her first album including how she created melodies with her brother.

Billie Eilish shows her a vision of her life, the experiences that she had went through, and all of the struggles that she had to endure. Haunting the narrative with her unique style and music.
