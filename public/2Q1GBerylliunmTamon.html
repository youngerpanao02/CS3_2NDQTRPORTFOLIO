Olivia Rodrigo: Disney star to Pop princess

Olivia Rodrigo, born on February 20, 2003 in California, USA, is a renowned Filipino-American singer-songwriter and actress. She started her career as an actress as a child, starring in many Disney shows and films --- she is best known for her role in the Disney series High School Musical as well as her debut album SOUR which kicked off her career as a singer. As years grew by, she rose to fame, topping the charts with various hits such as Driver's License and Good 4 u.

Though she has a small discography, considering her debut is only 4 years ago, she is nonetheless loved by many and inspire others with her songs and writing. Her latest album GUTS exceeded the expectations and made her more known globally.

As a global popstar, Olivia does not only showcase her talents to the world, but she also became an icon and voice for many as she takes stand and speaks up over global issues. Through her writing, she is able to express her emotions, thoughts, and feelings while being relatable to others, especially young teenagers.
